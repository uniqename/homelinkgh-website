1. Introduction

Welcome to HomeLinkGH ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered services platform mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

HomeLinkGH is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your personal information. This policy applies to all users of our platform in Ghana and the diaspora.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture
  • Ghana Card Information: Ghana Card number and verification status (for identity verification)
  • Location Data: GPS location, delivery addresses, and service locations
  • Payment Information: Payment methods and transaction history (processed securely)

2.2 AI Personalization Data

  • Preferences: Food preferences, dietary restrictions, service preferences
  • Behavioral Data: App usage patterns, search history, and interaction data
  • AI Learning Data: Personalized recommendations and learning patterns

2.3 Gamification Data

  • Activity Points: Points earned through app usage and referrals
  • Achievements: Badges, levels, and milestone completions
  • Engagement Metrics: Daily streaks, challenge participation

2.4 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Analytics: App performance, crash reports, and usage statistics
  • Network Information: IP address, network type, and connection data

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Process and fulfill food delivery and home service requests
  • Connect you with service providers and delivery partners
  • Provide customer support and resolve issues
  • Verify identity using Ghana Card information

3.2 AI-Powered Features

  • Personalize food and service recommendations
  • Optimize delivery routes and timing
  • Provide intelligent search and filtering
  • Enhance user experience through machine learning

3.3 Gamification and Engagement

  • Track activity points and achievements
  • Provide personalized challenges and rewards
  • Create engaging user experiences
  • Analyze usage patterns for improvement

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Service Providers

We share necessary information with:

  • Restaurants and Food Partners: Order details and delivery information
  • Service Providers: Service requests and location information
  • Payment Processors: Payment information for transaction processing
  • Delivery Partners: Delivery addresses and contact information

4.2 Third-Party Services

  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage data for app improvement
  • Cloud Services: Secure data storage and processing
  • Communication Services: Notifications and customer support

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Limited access to personal information
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Storage: Cloud infrastructure with enterprise-grade security

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Account Management

  • Access: View and update your personal information
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data

6.2 Privacy Controls

  • Location Sharing: Control location data sharing
  • AI Personalization: Opt out of AI-powered recommendations
  • Notifications: Manage push notifications and communications
  • Marketing: Opt out of promotional communications

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity
  • Transaction Data: 7 years for legal and tax compliance
  • AI Learning Data: Anonymized and retained for service improvement
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data retained indefinitely

8. Ghana Card Integration

Our Ghana Card verification system:

  • Verifies identity for enhanced security
  • Enables priority service access
  • Complies with Ghana's digital identity requirements
  • Stores verification status, not full card details

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or email.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Ghana. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

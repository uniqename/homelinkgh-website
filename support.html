Account & Registration

  • How do I create an account? Download the app and tap "Sign Up" to get started.
  • Can I use the app without registering? Some features are available as a guest, but registration provides the full experience.
  • How do I reset my password? Use "Forgot Password" on the login screen or contact support.

Booking Services

  • How do I book a service? Browse services, select a provider, choose your time, and confirm booking.
  • Can I cancel a booking? Yes, cancellation policies vary by service provider.
  • How do I track my service provider? Use the tracking feature in your active bookings.

Payments

  • What payment methods do you accept? Mobile money, bank cards, and cash payments.
  • Is my payment information secure? Yes, we use encrypted, secure payment processors.
  • How do refunds work? Refunds are processed according to our refund policy and provider terms.

Service Providers

  • How are service providers verified? All providers undergo background checks and skill verification.
  • What if I'm not satisfied with a service? Contact us immediately and we'll help resolve the issue.
  • Can I request a specific service provider? Yes, you can book with providers you've used before.
